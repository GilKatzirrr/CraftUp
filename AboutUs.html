Who We Are

CraftUp is a platform designed to bring people together through creativity. We help you find, join, or even create your own workshops – from arts and crafts to digital design, wellness, and beyond. Our mission is to empower community, learning, and self-expression through hands-on experiences.

Our Vision

At CraftUp, we believe that creativity should be accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our vision is to expand beyond local workshops – building digital tools that allow artists, creators, and curious minds to connect, teach, and inspire across the globe. We aim to become a vibrant ecosystem for learning, self-expression, and collaboration – both online and in-person.
